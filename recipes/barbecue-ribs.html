Barbecue-Ribs

Description

Barbecue Ribs are a mouthwatering masterpiece of slow-cooked meat, smoky flavor and sticky sweet-glaze.They are perfect for family gatherings weekend cookouts or just when you are creating something hearty

This recipes uses flavorful dry rub and a rich Barbecue sauce to make ribs that are juicy, fall-off-the-bone delicious and sure to impress your guest.

Ingredients

  • 4 pounds baby back pork ribs
  • 4 cloves garlic, sliced
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon paprika
  • 2 teaspoons salt
  • 2 teaspoons ground black pepper
  • 2 teaspoons chili pepper
  • 2 teaspoons ground cumin
  • 1/2 cup dark brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup cider vinegar
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup chili sauce
  • 1/4 cup chili sauce
  • 2 teaspoons chili pepper
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 tablespoons onion, chopped
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dry mustard
  • 1 clove crushed garlic

Steps

  1. Pre heat oven to 300 degrees F(150 degrees C).Place ribs on a rack in a shallow roasting pan. Scatter 4 cloves of sliced garlic over ribs. Cover, and bake for 2 1/2 hours. Cool slightly.
  2. In a small bowl, mix together white sugar, paprika, salt, black pepper, chili powder and ground cumin. Rub spices over cooled ribs. Cover and refrigerate overnight.
  3. In a small saucepan, mix together brown sugar, cider vinegar, ketchup, chili sauce, Worcestershire sauce, lemon juice, onion, dry mustard and 1 clove garlic. Simmer over medium-low heat, uncovered for 1 hour. Reserve a small amount for basting; the remainder is a dipping sauce.
  4. Preheat grill for medium heat.
  5. Place ribs on grill. Grill, covered, for about 12 minutes, basting with reserved sauce, until nicely browned and glazed. Serve with remaining sauce for dipping.
